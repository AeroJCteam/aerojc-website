All About Us

AeroJC is a student club started in 2016 by a team of seven students from one of the most reputed universities - JSSSTU in Karnataka.
As of now there are more than 40 students from across several disciplines of Engineering. The club organise events for the purpose of educating and training students in the field of aerodynamics.The club hosts an annual event where students get hands on experience on designing and modeling of a plane.
Teams also work on building hobby planes for better understanding of the fundamentals of the plane and its functionalities. The club also represents the university in both national and international events and participates in the annual competition hosted by SAE, for which the team analyzes, designs and fabricates a RC aircraft.
