Achievements

Every year the club participates in SAE International held in the USA, where teams all over the world compete for Aero Design competition
which is a real‐world design challenge and with each passing year the club is getting closer and closer to the top.
The team also competes in other national level competitions.

SAE Aero Design West 2016 - 9th in Micro Class.

SAE Aero Design West 2018 - 10th in Micro Class.

SAE Aero Design West 2018 - 3rd in Technical Presentation.

SAE Aero Design West 2019 - 6th in Micro Class.

SAE Aero Design West 2019 - 4th in Design Report.

SAE Aero Design West 2019 - 7th in both Technical Presentation and Design Report.

NITTE Aeromodelling Competition - 4th position.
